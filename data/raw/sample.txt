# HundredxMind AI Assistant - Sample Knowledge Base
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is powerful because it can process vast amounts of data quickly and learn from patterns.
Large Language Models (LLMs) like Llama 3.2 are a type of AI that can understand and generate human-like text.
Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) combines LLMs with external knowledge to provide accurate, up-to-date answers.
Our assistant uses RAG to answer questions based on your internal documents.
